How to read: follow each line left to right. Lines rising together suggest alignment; one line rising before another suggests a possible delay; broken lines mean that source was not captured.

Each dot is one day. Use the switches to test whether mood, curiosity, execution, and long-game integrity actually move together.

How to read: dots that climb from bottom-left to top-right suggest a positive relationship. A loose cloud means weak connection; lonely dots are outlier days worth inspecting.
