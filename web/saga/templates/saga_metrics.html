Evidence

Charts explain the analysis above. They are here to answer why, not make you do the diagnosis yourself.

Systems matrix

Daily is Quest output plus Pomo volume; Long Game is the Hard 90 day score; Evolution counts active/touched/started experiments minus verdict debt; Emotion blends pleasantness with lower mood load. A dash means that system had no signal that day.

Execution vs Long Game

Each dot is one day with both Quest/Pomo and Hard 90 data. Right means more daily execution; higher means stronger long-game integrity. The top-right zone is aligned work; bottom-right is productive drift.

Mood → Output/Integrity correlation

Each point is a captured mood day. X is average Saga pleasantness from -5 to +5; Y is either Daily Output or Hard 90 integrity. This shows whether unpleasant mood coincides with weaker execution, weaker long-game follow-through, or neither.

Focus quality

Pomos, interruptions, hollow sessions, and berserker sessions by day.

Experiment runway

Active trials, logged/touched trials, and verdict debt across the grain.

Bucket risk by mood

Counts Hard 90 task entries that were not completed, grouped by bucket and by whether that day's Saga mood averaged pleasant or unpleasant. These are misses/partials, not days, so several can appear in one week.
